    How to play

    • Build: merge 2 to 32 normally; reaching 64 starts a 5-turn warning, then betrayal
    • Bombs: show both the tile value and a countdown
    • Defuse: slide a matching tile into a bomb - the bomb vanishes, your tile stays
    • Timers: higher value = more time - 64/70s, 128/90s, 256/110s, 512/135s, 1024/165s, 2048/200s
    • Rewards: defuse XP, combos, chapter stars, captain rank-ups
